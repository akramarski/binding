A Journey into the Future of Interactive Publishing

Navigating the Past, Present, and Future of Print. Uncover the transformative journey from traditional to avant-garde, as we explore post-digital print production, platforming books, and the boundless future of print creation—an immersive odyssey into the ever-evolving narrative of digital innovation.

Immerse yourself in the captivating realm of digital publishing with our carefully curated collection of literary works, featuring "Post-Digital Print" by Alessandro Ludovico, "Platforming Books" by Craig Mod, and "Unbuilding" by Rob Giampietro. Explore how these masterpieces challenge traditional conventions, delve into novel narrative forms, and contemplate the evolution of reading in the digital age. Our exhibition delves into the convergence of art, technology, and literature, providing a unique perspective on the metamorphosis of books into dynamic platforms and interactive experiences. Step into the future of printed words at our digital exhibition—a fusion of innovation, creativity, and the written word. Welcome to a new chapter in the world of literature.
